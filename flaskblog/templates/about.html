About us


"Khana Khojana" is actually a nepali name where the word 'Khana' means food and 'Khojana' means to search which makes our project name very meaningful to 'search food' . It is meant for the users who are searching for different recipes to make with the ingredients they have in their fridge. And also for the users to search the restaurants to have foods of a particular cuisine. Not only this, but we also allow our users to discuss and post or present their opinions regarding restaurants or recipes in our dashboard!
